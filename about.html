About AV Repair

Since 1988, AV Repair has proudly served the community with expert auto body repair, mechanical services, paint restoration, and more. Our dedicated team of technicians combines years of experience with a passion for precision and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

Our goal is simple — to ensure your complete satisfaction, whether you visit for regular maintenance or extensive repairs. We believe that our reputation is built through word of mouth, and we strive to earn your trust with every job we complete.

“Jim and his team are my go-to auto service shop. I’ve been taking my cars to him for over 25 years. They’ve done everything from body work to engine repair. I wouldn’t think of taking a vehicle of mine anywhere else.”
— Paul M., Andover, MA

We take pride in forming long-term relationships with our customers and treating each repair with the attention it deserves. At AV Repair, our promise is to deliver honest, high-quality work without inflated prices. We offer complete bumper-to-bumper service using trusted parts and fluids so you can drive with confidence.

Our Team

Our team is led by Jim Morrison, a seasoned professional with over three decades of experience in the auto repair industry. Alongside him is a skilled group of ASE-certified technicians who are continually trained on the latest automotive technologies and repair techniques. Together, we make sure that every vehicle we service meets our highest standards of quality, performance, and safety.
